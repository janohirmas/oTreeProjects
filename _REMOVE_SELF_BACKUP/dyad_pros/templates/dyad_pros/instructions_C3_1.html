Your task (II)

The task has multiple rounds. Each round of the task goes as follows:

  1. You will see a number, between 1 and 6, appearing on the computer screen. The number that will appear on the screen is generated by another participant in the room who is engaging in a different task. This is not the person you are paired with. On a later page you will learn how this number is generated.
  2. After that, you will roll a die and will be asked to report the die roll outcome.
  3. At the end of each round, you will see the number that appeared on the screen, your own report, and the payoffs associated with that round.

On the next page you will learn how the payoffs are determined.
