Numbers appearing on the screen (III)

In each round of your task, you will see the number that a participant in the role of the “first mover” reported in that round.

Note that:

  1. Your behavior does not affect the payoff of the first mover, nor the payoffs of the second mover.
  2. Your behavior only affects the person you are paired with.
  3. The other participants are not informed about your reports in the task.
