Payoff of the participants

If the first mover and the second mover report the same number (“a double”) both are paid according to the following table:

First mover's report Second mover's report First mover's payoff Second mover's payoff
1 1 1 Euro 1 Euro
2 2 2 Euro 2 Euro
3 3 3 Euro 3 Euro
4 4 4 Euro 4 Euro
5 5 5 Euro 5 Euro
6 6 6 Euro 6 Euro


If the first mover and the second mover report two different numbers (“no double”) both earn 0 euros.

The task has multiple rounds. At the end of the experiment, one round will be randomly selected to be paid out. The first and second movers' payoff in part 1 will be determined by their reports in that round.
