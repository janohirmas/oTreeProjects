Your task

In part 1, you are a passive participant.

This means that you can not affect your own payoff in part 1. Your payoff in part 1 depends on the behavior of another participant in the room, and will be between 0 and 6 euros. You will learn about the amount you earn in part 1 at the end of the experiment.

In the meantime, we will ask you to guess how participants behave in two tasks. Both of these task do not affect your payoff. For that, you will first read about one task some participants engaged in, and guess how they behaved in that task. Then you will read about a second task other participants engage in, and guess how they behave in that task.
