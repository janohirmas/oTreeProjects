Task of the other participants

Participants are paired in groups of 2 and they keep the same partner throughout the task.

One of the two participants in a pair is assigned to the role of the “first mover” and the other is assigned to the role of the “second mover”. Participants keep their role throughout the task.

The task has multiple rounds. Each round of the task goes as follows:

  1. The first mover rolls a die and is asked to report the die roll outcome.
  2. After that, the second mover sees the number the first mover reported.
  3. Then, the second mover rolls a die and is asked to report the die roll outcome.
  4. At the end of each round, the first mover and the second mover see both reported outcomes and the payoff associated with that round.

On the next page you will learn how the payoff is determined.
