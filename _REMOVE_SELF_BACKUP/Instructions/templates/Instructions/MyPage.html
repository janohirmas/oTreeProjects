Step 1

In the following study you are about to make decisions of whether to accept a lottery or not. These lotteries will give you with equal probabilities (fifty-fifty) a positive or negative outcome. You are going to choose whether to accept or reject one a total of 48 different lotteries. The overall duration of the study should be of no more than 10-15 minutes. Each outcome in these lotteries consists of a positive or negative amount of points (1 point = 0.19 EUR). In the end, one of these lotteries will be randomly chosen and added (or subtracted) to your initial show-up fee, that you received for participating in this study.
